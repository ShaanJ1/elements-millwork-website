About Us

Overview

We specialize in creating custom kitchen cabinets, closets, and desks tailored to your needs. Our expertise also includes high-quality cabinetry for commercial spaces like restaurants and offices.

Additionally, we wholesale a wide range of flooring materials, including laminate, vinyl, carpet, and tiles, to suit any residential or commercial project.

With a strong commitment to quality, we ensure every project meets the highest standards, providing durable and stylish solutions.

Our History

Elements Millwork started in 2016 is a family-owned and operated business. Over the past eight years, it has grown while maintaining its family-focused values. The close-knit operation has contributed to its steady success and commitment to quality.
